Algorithm to prepare Biscuits 


Step 1: Take the ingredients like Sugar, Butter, Egg, Flour.
Step 2: Firstly mix Cream Butter and Sugar. 
Step 3: Now beat eggs into creamed butter and sugar.
Step 4: Add Dry ingredients like flour and baking powder to wet paste.
Step 5:	Add some nuts and chocos. 
Step 6: Shape the Biscuits now.
Step 7:	Bake them and then let them cool.
